Best players on UEFA Champions League 2023 - 2024

How we measure?

We check how each player performs compared to the top 10% average by using percentages, using fbref.com data.

Higher percentages mean the player is performing closer or above the top 10% average.

For example: If a player has 125% participation in goals, it means they are 25% above the average of the top 10% with the highest goal participation.




What are we looking for?

If a player is individually skilled there should be a relation between his offensive actions and goals or danger involvement.

We're looking for the players with the highest goal-action relation.
