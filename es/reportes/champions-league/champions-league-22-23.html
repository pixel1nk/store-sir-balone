Another week has passed and here are the top performers in attack, defense and goal keeping for all the top 5 European Leagues

How we measure?

We check how each player performs compared to the top 10% average by using percentages.

Higher percentages mean the player is performing closer or above the top 10% average.

Top 5 Runners

A continuous ball control by one player attempting to draw the team significantly closer to the opponent goal.

A run is considered progressive if the distance before the starting point and the last touch of the player is:

at least 30 meters closer to opponent goal if starting and finishing points are in own half

at least 15 meters closer to opponent goal if starting and finishing points are in different field halves

at least 10 meters closer to opponent goal if starting and finishing points are in opponent half

Odds: The higher the number, higher the probability that after a run it ends in goal or danger

Goal Impact: Non penalty goals + Assits + Second Assist (assisting the assistant)

Danger means: If the action ends in Shots + Shot assist (a pass followed by a shot), + Key pass (a pass that leads to a goal or a close-to-goal action)
